Our Story

Marksys was established in 2009, in the city of Khobar – the eastern province of the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia (KSA) Since our launch in the Saudi market, we have specialized in the marketing sector, specifically in the field of supplying promotional corporate Gifts Items from various international brands such as WATERMAN, MONT BLANC, ST DUPONT, CROSS, and many other brands, in addition to design, create & manufacturing unique gifts ideas custom-made that reflect our clients cooperates identity and culture, which will leave a lasting impression. Moreover, supplying promotional apparel, providing our services to companies operating in the energy sector, especially the field of oil, gas, and petrochemicals, since we are located in the city of Khobar near one of the largest oil companies in the world, Saudi Aramco company, also there are many local, international foreign companies which provides services in the field of drilling and oil exploration in the same region. After 3 years, we sought to develop and expand our business, we established a new business division specialized in supplying uniforms and professional work wear due to the increasing demand in the region, and we started searching for sources of professional fire-resistant clothing and Personal Safety Equipment (PPE) with high specifications Due to its importance for companies operating in the petroleum sector. After our success in this filed, the management decided to expand in the field of uniforms and work wear provide its services to companies in other sectors such as the commercial sector, industrial sector, Transportation & logistics Service sector, Facilities Management services sector, Restaurant chains, food and beverage service sector, and the health sector. And many other commercials, industrial and services sectors. This required searching for new sources of fabrics and clothes to serve these different sectors and provide their needs, and accordingly, a contract was signed with Tootal Fabrics – Holland as an exclusive agent for its products for all types of high-quality fabrics distinguished by their diversity for more than 3000 different categories with storage quantities always available and ready to ship up to 10 million square meters, with a variety of materials in different colors. In addition, a distribution of other international products such as TENCATE, HONEYWELL, 3M, CARRINGTON, PRIME CAPTAIN, KOMY and many of the strongest international companies in the field of garment & PPE supplying professional clothing and personal safety protective. All of this is to improve our services for customers in order to meet their different needs and reach the best levels of service in this field.
